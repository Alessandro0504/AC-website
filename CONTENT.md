# Content & Voice

## Who AC is

Alessandro Cordano — institutional commodity broker, **Geneva, est. 2018**. Sources premium agricultural commodities (coffee, cacao, frozen fruit, specialty produce) directly from verified primary producers across the **Americas, MENA, and Asia-Pacific**, for institutional buyers under audited contracts.

## Voice

**Editorial · institutional · understated.** Closer to a quarterly investor letter than a marketing site. Lean on:

- Latinate registration marks: `MMXXVI`, `ISS. MMXXVI · № 01`, `◆ Plate · subject`
- Compliance vocabulary: KYC / AML, EUDR Aligned, Marine Cargo Insured, Geneva Counsel
- Geographic register: LATAM-Origin, APAC-Demand, EU-Compliance, MENA-Logistics
- Editorial flourishes set in `<I>` italic (Instrument Serif), e.g. *"Sourcing integrity, at origin."*

Avoid: exclamation marks, growth-hacky CTAs, emoji, casual register, "we" overuse.
